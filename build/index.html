Our Mission and Values

Style Acre provides safe and nurturing environments for people with learning disabilities and autism to grow and develop in their own unique way and with the utmost dignity. We recognise and embrace individuality and deliver person-centred care of the highest standard. Our values are important to us, and we use them to recruit, train and manage our staff, and support the people using our services.

  • Caring

    Treating everyone with dignity, compassion and kindness

  • Respectful

    At all times and for everyone

  • Honesty

    To ensure we develop and maintain trusting relationships

  • Ambition

    To ensure people have choice and control and the opportunity to fulfil their potential

  • Collaborative

    Working with others to achieve our mission and vision
